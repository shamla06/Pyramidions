Our Association Models

Different Needs,
Different Models

Your project needs are unique and agile. So why force fit them to align the standard workflow and billing methods? At Pyramidion, we offer three different choices of engagement models that better suit different types of project scope.

Fixed Bid

For projects with a clearly defined structure and end-goal, we provide you with a fixed estimate in terms of both the pricing and timeline.


Time & Material

Want to get started on a development project where the scope can expand or reduce? We can make it work for you by billing only for actual time spent on the project by allocated resources.

Team Extension

We have project-ready designers and developers on our payroll with versatile skill sets ranging from UX design to project management to languages such as React, Flutter and Angular.
